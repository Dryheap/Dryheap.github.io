Analyzing the 2021-2022 NBA Season

Team Analysis

Select a team from the Bar Chart to display that team on the associated Radar Chart. You can make multiple selections to compare their statistics.
Selecting a team will also filter the Scatter Plot below to display only players for that selected team.

Player Analysis

Select a player from the Scatter Plot to display that player on the associated Radar Chart. You can make multiple selections to compare their statistics.
